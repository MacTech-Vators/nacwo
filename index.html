Background

The National Association of Car Wash Operators was first established in 2012. Its formation and establishment were prompted by a need to organize the car wash operators sector. The sector has not been organized and properly regulated despite its role in job creation and crime reduction. NACWO has been established to address all the challenges and provide solutions to the sector.
